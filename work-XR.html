An XR Exhibition Design Proposal @Lenovo

The on-site exhibition displays 4 historical photos documenting the life of the shadow puppet Wu Song. Equipped with XR glasses and guided by Lenovo Daystar, visitors are transported back in time to immerse themselves in the world of shadow puppetry. From creating props and performing the play "Wu Song Fights the Tiger," to witnessing its decline and revival, visitors will explore the fascinating stories behind the scenes.
